Pokemon Stat and Type Explorer

Picking individual Pokemon, as well as the types of Pokemon one would want on their starting team can be difficult.
There are plenty of stats that make the choices difficult. Whether it is selecting based off type or specific stats,
this data visualization will help to narrow your choices based on the direction of team you want to build.

Average Stats by Type

If you prioritize a specific stat (Attack, Defense, HP, etc.), then considering Pokemon type is a must.
Some types have naturally higher base stats than others. For example, if you select defense, you will
see that rock type has a higher defense stat on average than any other type. Therefore, if you want to
build your team around a specific stat, examining this chart will help.
Most times when trying to maximize a stat for a team, one should observe how an increase in one stat can
affect other stats. To avoid over specializing, one should pick at least one other stat to try to work toward
when creating their team. This chart allows comparison of different stats for every pokemon, filtered by type.
Pokemon that excel in both stats selected will be found in the upper right of the graph. Since you have
potentially already selected a type from the last graph. Now you can compare all the Poemon within any
specific type.
Hover Over Datapoint to Display Pokemon Name
Finally, let's take a look at the best overall Pokemon. Without looking at meximizing a specific stat, by looking
at the sum of a Pokemon's stats, we can compare the best overall Pokemon. These Pokemon may not excel in any particular
stat, but are the best all-around Pokemon if one is curious about maximizing ALL stats, not any specific stat. One can
toggle through the top 100 Pokemon ranked by total stats to compare with the choices made from the previous two charts.
